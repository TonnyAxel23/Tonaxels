Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

General Questions

What services does Tonaxels Technology offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of digital services including custom web development, mobile app development (both iOS and Android), UI/UX design, digital marketing, cloud solutions, and ongoing maintenance and support for your digital products.

Where is Tonaxels Technology located?

Our headquarters are based in Kenya, but we serve clients globally. Our distributed team allows us to work with businesses from anywhere in the world.

How do I get started with a project?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us through our website, email, or phone. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and timeline. From there, we'll provide a proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and cost.

Project Process

What is your development process?

We follow an agile development process that typically includes these stages:

  1. Discovery and requirements gathering
  2. Planning and architecture
  3. Design (UI/UX)
  4. Development
  5. Testing and quality assurance
  6. Deployment
  7. Maintenance and support

We maintain transparent communication throughout the entire process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, features, and scope. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

Will I be involved during the development process?

Absolutely! We believe collaboration is key to success. You'll have regular opportunities to review progress, provide feedback, and request adjustments. We schedule weekly check-ins and provide access to development milestones for your review.

Pricing & Payments

How do you determine project pricing?

We provide custom quotes based on project scope, complexity, and timeline. Factors we consider include:

  • Project size and features
  • Design requirements
  • Technology stack
  • Integration needs
  • Ongoing maintenance requirements

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials engagement models depending on project needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa), credit/debit cards, and PayPal. Payment terms are typically structured with an initial deposit, milestone payments during development, and a final payment upon project completion.

Do you offer maintenance packages after launch?

Yes, we offer several maintenance and support packages tailored to your needs. These can include:

  • Regular updates and security patches
  • Bug fixes and troubleshooting
  • Performance optimization
  • Content updates
  • Hosting and server management

We'll discuss these options as part of our project planning.

Still have questions?

We're happy to answer any other questions you might have about our services or processes.
